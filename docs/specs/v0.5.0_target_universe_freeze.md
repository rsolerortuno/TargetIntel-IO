# v0.5.0 target-universe freeze

Issue 504 defines three non-interchangeable immutable universes: a curated
benchmark for later validation, a pre-DepMap discovery universe for later
prioritization, and a release-pinned full-matrix background for statistical
context. Their type participates in identity, while row/source/axis ordering
does not. A freeze is reproducibility metadata, not validation, benchmark
success, or clinical utility.

The benchmark is frozen as
`contexts/melanoma_anti_pd1/benchmarks/benchmark_v1.tsv`, derived from the
audited 56-symbol internal configuration. Its class distribution is: 21
`known_positive`, 12 `biomarker_not_intervention_target`, 10
`context_dependent`, 5 `mechanism_control`, 5 `challenging_control`, and 3
`negative_control`. Entries carry controlled class, qualitative behaviour,
role, evidence key, rationale, curation version, canonical resistance-axis
key, limitations, and deterministic development/holdout partition. It stores
no expected numerical rank, score, dependency value, probability, or
selectivity result. Holdout labels are not serialized into discovery records.

Resistance-axis keys exactly reuse `configs/resistance_axes.yaml`:
`checkpoint_redundancy`, `antigen_presentation_loss`, `ifng_resistance`,
`myeloid_suppression`, `metabolic_immune_suppression`, `tgfb_caf_exclusion`,
`melanoma_plasticity`, `tumor_intrinsic_driver`, `immune_cold_state`, and
`treg_suppression`; `other_unresolved` is the explicit non-axis state.

Discovery construction accepts only explicit source records governed by an
immutable `DiscoveryUniversePolicy`. The current 66-gene artifact is
`contexts/melanoma_anti_pd1/universes/discovery_sources_v1.tsv` plus its
required benchmark union; its seed records are the explicit example genes from
the canonical resistance-axis configuration. It recursively rejects DepMap
outcomes, profiles, rank/score fields, benchmark outcomes, credentials and
hidden reasoning. Unknown source classes fail closed. Benchmark union is policy
controlled. Unresolved and rejected records remain separately emitted.

Background membership comes only from valid canonical identities in the Issue
502 full-matrix `gene_index.tsv`, with release-manifest and mapping identities.
Malformed columns and conflicting duplicate labels remain reported; absence
from the background means absent from pinned matrix scope, not biological
irrelevance.

The freeze example produces canonical JSON and stable TSVs for all three
universes, overlap, unresolved/rejected records and a leakage audit. It makes
no score, rank, profile, modality, role, card, report, network, subprocess or
clinical calculation. The synthetic fixture is non-biological. Issue 505 must
consume a frozen compatible manifest and may evaluate only after this freeze.
